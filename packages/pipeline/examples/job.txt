About Us
--------
Nimbus Technologies builds cloud-native observability tools used by
engineering teams at over 400 companies worldwide. Our platform ingests
billions of events per day and turns them into actionable insights,
helping SREs and developers reduce incident response time by 60%.
Founded in 2019 and backed by Series C funding, we are growing fast
and looking for talented engineers to shape the next generation of our
distributed data pipeline.

The Role
--------
We are looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our Core Platform
team. You will design, build, and operate the high-throughput ingestion
and query services that power our product. This is a hands-on role
where you will split your time between writing code, reviewing designs,
and mentoring junior engineers.

Responsibilities
----------------
- Design and implement scalable microservices in Go and Rust
- Own end-to-end delivery of features from RFC to production rollout
- Improve system reliability, targeting 99.95% uptime SLAs
- Participate in on-call rotation (roughly one week per quarter)
- Collaborate with Product and Design to refine requirements
- Conduct code reviews and contribute to internal engineering standards
- Mentor mid-level and junior engineers through pairing and tech talks

Requirements
------------
- 5+ years of professional software engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in Go, Rust, or a similar systems language
- Deep understanding of distributed systems (consensus, partitioning,
  replication)
- Experience operating services on Kubernetes in production
- Solid grasp of observability: metrics, traces, and structured logging
- Comfortable with CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions, ArgoCD, or similar)
- Excellent written and verbal communication in English

Preferred Qualifications
------------------------
- Experience with Apache Kafka, NATS, or Pulsar
- Familiarity with ClickHouse, TimescaleDB, or other OLAP databases
- Contributions to open-source projects
- Public speaking or technical writing track record
- Working knowledge of German (any level)
